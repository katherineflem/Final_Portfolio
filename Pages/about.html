My name is Katherine Fleming and I am an aspiring developer and graphic designer in my hometown of New Orleans, Louisiana.I have always been creatively inclined and even started out my college journey at LSU as a graphic design major, and ended up receiving minors in Fine Arts, Mass Communications and Sociology. After college, my interests led me to web design. I moved to Birmingham, Alabama and immersed myself in the tech scene there by enrolling in a coding bootcamp and becoming a full-stack developer while continuing to work a full time job in non-profit. Upon moving back to New Orleans, I wanted to keep learning and gain more confidence in my skills. I enrolled in a Web Design Course and Java Developement Course through Tech Talent South and look forward to the opportunities it brings in New Orleans!
